ifly

An airline app with a smooth booking experience.

A solo UX design project for the UX Design Institute and the Galsgow Caledonian Uninversity.

Project Overview

The simplest part of planning a trip is deciding the destination. But booking a flight through a mobile app can be a complex experience. Through this case study I propose a booking flow with a friction free experience.

My Role

This case study was my graduation project for the UX design institute. Throughout this project I performed both the research and design process in order to achieve the professional diploma.
